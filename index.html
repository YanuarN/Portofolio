Mendhologi Farm Website

I am developing a website designed to assist farmers in marketing their livestock. The platform is being built using Laravel, a powerful PHP framework known for its scalability and robust features. The goal is to streamline the selling process, making it more efficient and accessible for farmers, ultimately helping them reach a broader market and increase their sales.
